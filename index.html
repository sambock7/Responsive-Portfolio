About Me

Hi, my name is Sam. I am 29 years old and live in Ridgewood, Queens. I work in digital marketing, currently, but aspire to be a full-stack developer so I am attending a coding bootcamp at Columbia University for 6 months. I am not sure which area of development I want to pursue yet, but I'm hoping I will figure it out as I go along. I know I am interested in making my ideas come to life and development will make that happen.

A little about my background, I grew up in New Rochelle, NY and I was passionate about playing softball and basketball. I later went to college at Arizona State University and majored in Marketing and minored in Film and Media Productions.

My first major career move was working for the Arizona Diamondbacks as their social media manager, responsible for all content on their Twitter, Facebook and Instagram. I moved on to Elio Motors where I was a marketing director and then worked for several startups and tech companies afterwards.
